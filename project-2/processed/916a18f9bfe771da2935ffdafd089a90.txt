Using Special Collections
Request special collections items using the “Request to Scan or Visit” link in the HOLLIS record or through HOLLIS Special Request . Before your visit, schedule a Reading Room appointment . Please schedule your visit at least 24 hours in advance, as no walk-in or same-day appointments are available. Feel free to contact us to confirm availability ahead of your visit.
We encourage instructors to schedule class or section visit to the Special Collections Reading Room for hands-on engagement with our materials. To learn more, contact us directly.
