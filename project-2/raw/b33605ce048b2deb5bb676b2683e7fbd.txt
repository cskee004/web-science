								<h6>Whole Home</h6>
								<ul>
									<li>Kick your shoes off at the door</li>
									<li>Bring in fresh air</li>
									<li>Install detectors for smoke and ‘the silent killer’</li>
									<li>(Re)connect with nature and natural light indoors</li>
									<li>Get the lead out (for homes built before 1980)</li>
								</ul>
							</div>
						</div>	
					</a>
					<a href="https://homes.forhealth.org/36-expert-tips-for-a-healthier-home/bedrooms/" class="area bedroom" data-tooltip-content="#tooltip-bedroom">
						<div class="circle"></div>
						<div class="element-hidden">
							<div id="tooltip-bedroom" class="tooltip-content">
								<h6>Bedroom</h6>
								<ul>
									<li>Train your brain and make this the sleeproom</li>
									<li>Black out the room (and ‘blue-out’ your lights)</li>
									<li>Treat the air (and yourself)</li>
									<li>Keep your cool at night</li>
									<li>Block out the noise</li>
								</ul>
							</div>
						</div>	
					</a>
					<a href="https://homes.forhealth.org/36-expert-tips-for-a-healthier-home/bathrooms/" class="area bathroom" data-tooltip-content="#tooltip-bathroom">
						<div class="circle"></div>
						<div class="element-hidden">
							<div id="tooltip-bathroom" class="tooltip-content">
								<h6>Bathroom</h6>
								<ul>
									<li>Control moisture by exhausting air outdoors</li>
									<li>Limit the use of air fresheners</li>
									<li>Detoxify cleaners and personal care products</li>
									<li>Skip the antimicrobials</li>
									<li>Prevent slips, trips and falls with handrails and non-slip mats</li>
								</ul>
							</div>
						</div>	
					</a>
					<a href="https://homes.forhealth.org/36-expert-tips-for-a-healthier-home/living-room/" class="area living-room" data-tooltip-content="#tooltip-living-room">
						<div class="circle"></div>
						<div class="element-hidden">
							<div id="tooltip-living-room" class="tooltip-content">
								<h6>Living Room</h6>
								<ul>
									<li>Vacuum. Regularly. With HEPA.</li>
									<li>Don’t smoke indoors (better yet, don’t smoke at all)</li>
									<li>Stamp out the candles and incense</li>
									<li>Choose furniture and carpets without harmful chemicals</li>
									<li>Properly vent fireplaces and woodstoves</li>
								</ul>
							</div>
						</div>	
					</a>
					<a href="https://homes.forhealth.org/36-expert-tips-for-a-healthier-home/kitchen/" class="area kitchen" data-tooltip-content="#tooltip-kitchen">
						<div class="circle"></div>
						<div class="element-hidden">
							<div id="tooltip-kitchen" class="tooltip-content">
								<h6>Kitchen</h6>
								<ul>
									<li>Cook with the exhaust hood on (and vented outdoors)</li>
									<li>Keep a fire extinguisher within easy reach</li>
									<li>Filter your drinking water where necessary</li>
									<li>Control pests using IPM, not more pesticides</li>
									<li>Choose glassware and cast iron or ceramic cookware</li>
								</ul>
							</div>
						</div>	
					</a>
					<a href="https://homes.forhealth.org/36-expert-tips-for-a-healthier-home/basement/" class="area basement" data-tooltip-content="#tooltip-basement">
						<div class="circle"></div>
						<div class="element-hidden">
							<div id="tooltip-basement" class="tooltip-content">
								<h6>Basement</h6>
								<ul>
									<li>Measure and control radon</li>
									<li>Do not disturb signs of asbestos</li>
									<li>Dehumidify and inspect for signs of water issues</li>
									<li>Choose a hard floor</li>
									<li>Solve the solvent storage issue</li>
								</ul>
							</div>
						</div>	
					</a>						
				</div>
			</div>
