<section class="su-card__contents">
                  <h2>About the Vice Provost</h2>
                  <p><strong>Michele Rasmussen became Stanford University’s Vice Provost for Student Affairs on September 10, 2024. </strong>As vice provost, she oversees the Student Affairs division that manages residential life and student experiences outside the classroom. Its units include the <a href="https://studentaffairs.stanford.edu/about-student-affairs/student-affairs-units/centers-equity-community-leadership">Centers for Equity, Community, and Leadership</a>, <a href="https://studentaffairs.stanford.edu/about-student-affairs/student-affairs-units/dean-students">Dean of Students</a>, <a href="https://studentaffairs.stanford.edu/about-student-affairs/student-affairs-units/inclusion-community-integrative-learning">Inclusion, Community &amp; Integrative Learning</a>, <a href="https://studentaffairs.stanford.edu/about-student-affairs/student-affairs-units/learning-technologies-spaces">Learning Technologies &amp; Space</a>, <a href="https://studentaffairs.stanford.edu/about-student-affairs/student-affairs-units/student-academic-services">Student &amp; Academic Services</a>, and <a href="https://studentaffairs.stanford.edu/about-student-affairs/student-affairs-units/vaden-health-services">Vaden Health Services</a>. She is also a member of the University Cabinet.</p>
