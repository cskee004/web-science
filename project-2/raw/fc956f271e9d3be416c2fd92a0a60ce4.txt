      <h2 class="block-title" ng-non-bindable="">Our Mission</h2>
  
  <div class="block-content content" ng-non-bindable=""><div id='boxes-box-1407116872' class='boxes-box'><div class="boxes-box-content"><p>The Mineralogical &amp; Geological Museum at Harvard University (MGMH) is committed to the development and preservation of world-class collections of minerals, rocks, ores, meteorites and gems for research, education, and public display. We strive to meet the needs of students and faculty at Harvard University as well the geological community and public at large by serving as a uniquely rich resource of materials and information.</p></div></div></div>
