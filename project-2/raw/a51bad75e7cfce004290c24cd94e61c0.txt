                    <h1 id="page-title" ng-non-bindable="">
                      Botany Libraries                    </h1>
                </header>

								<div id="content-panels" class="at-panel gpanel panel-display content clearfix">
									<div class="region region-content-top"><div class="region-inner clearfix"><div id="block-boxes-1390834806" class="block block-boxes block-boxes-os_boxes_html no-title"  module="boxes" delta="1390834806"><div class="block-inner clearfix">  
  
  <div class="block-content content" ng-non-bindable=""><div id='boxes-box-1390834806' class='boxes-box'><div class="boxes-box-content"><p>
	<br />The Harvard University Herbaria house five comprehensive, non-circulating research libraries that are managed collectively as the Botany Libraries. The combined collections are rich repositories of rare books, manuscripts, field notes, and historical correspondence, as well as current monographs, journals and electronic media.
</p>

<p>
	The Libraries support the academic and research interests of the faculty, researchers, staff and students of the Harvard University Herbaria and are open to an international community of scholars.
</p>

<p>
	Search <a href="http:/hollis.harvard.edu/" title="">HOLLIS</a> the Harvard University On-Line Library catalog.
